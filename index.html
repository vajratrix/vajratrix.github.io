Who We Are

Three Forces,
United as One

Vajratrix Group is the convergence of three formidable entities — Chinmoy Tech, Sayantika Group, and Pathak Info — forming an indestructible alliance in technology, strategy, and information services.


Rooted in Sanskrit tradition, our name carries a hidden truth: Vajra (indestructible force) + Trix (matrix of power). Together, we deliver end-to-end solutions that build trust, drive growth, and create lasting impact across industries.
